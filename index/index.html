Say Anything 
Anonymous Open Forum

All text responses in this forum will be collected, printed, and molded into a public art piece to be installed on campus. In the meantime, this site will update and display responses, artwork (email: artist@example.com), and archived zines.

All responses are anonymous; however, you may leave your name if you'd like.

You may respond with any text, in any language, as many times as you want.

Text will be printed verbatim with no alterations.

Hate speech will not be included in the piece; however, profanity is accepted.

Intellectual Property Agreement:

By responding to this form, I agree to that my response may be incorporated verbatim into a public art piece.
